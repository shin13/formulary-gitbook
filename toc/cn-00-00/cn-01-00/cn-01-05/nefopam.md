# Nefopam

## Capain 30mg

|:-------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Indications        | Relief of pain                                                                             |
| Dosing             | 30-60 mg 3 times daily.                                                                    |
| Hepatic Impairment | Dose adjustment not necessary                                                              |

| Pregnancy          | Unknown                                                                                    |
