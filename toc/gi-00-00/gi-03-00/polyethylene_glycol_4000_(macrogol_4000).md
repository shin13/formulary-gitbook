# Polyethylene Glycol 4000 (Macrogol 4000)

## Forlax 10gm/pack

| TAH Drug Code      | [OFORL](https://www.tahsda.org.tw/drugs/hissearch.php?drug_code=OFORL)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|:-------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Indications        | Constipation for children older than 8 years and adolescents.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Dosing             | Oral: Usual dose: 1 to 2 sachets (10 to 20 gm) daily, preferably in the morning; in some patients, a dose of 1 sachet (10 gm) every other day may be sufficient.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| Hepatic Impairment | Dose adjustment not necessary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Renal Impairment   | Dose adjustment not necessary                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Contraindications  | Hypersensitivity to polyethylene glycol or any component of the formulation; severe inflammatory bowel disease (eg, ulcerative colitis, Crohn disease) or toxic megacolon associated with symptomatic stenosis; obstructed bowel; stomach pain of unknown cause. Digestive perforation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Adverse Effects    | >10%: Gastrointestinal: Bloating (<=50%), gastrointestinal fullness (<=50%), nausea (<=50%) 1% to 10%: Gastrointestinal: Abdominal distension, abdominal pain, diarrhea Frequency not defined: Cardiovascular: Asystole (older adults) Dermatologic: Dermatitis, perianal skin irritation, urticaria Endocrine & metabolic: Dehydration (older adults), electrolyte disturbance (including hypokalemia, hyponatremia) Gastrointestinal: Abdominal cramps, borborygmi, esophageal perforation (older adults), vomiting Hematologic & oncologic: Upper gastrointestinal hemorrhage (older adults; from Mallory-Weiss tear) Hypersensitivity: Anaphylaxis Respiratory: Dyspnea (older adults with pulmonary edema; sudden), rhinorrhea <1%, postmarketing, and/or case reports: Bowel urgency, fecal incontinence, hypersensitivity reaction (including anaphylactic shock, angioedema, facial edema, pruritus, skin rash, urticaria) |
