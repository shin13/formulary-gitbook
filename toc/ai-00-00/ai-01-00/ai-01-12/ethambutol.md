|:-------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

| Dosing             | 15-25 mg/kg orally once daily.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
